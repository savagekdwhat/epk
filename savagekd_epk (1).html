About

$avage KD is a Melbourne-based R&B and soul artist crafting music that lives in the late-night space between vulnerability and control. His sound is built on dark ambient production, heavy low-end, and vocal deliveries that move between quiet confession and cold assurance — emotionally direct, sonically immersive.

Drawing from heartbreak, ego, and introspection, $avage KD's catalog has accumulated over 1.1M streams across Spotify, with listeners across Australia, the US, the UK, and New Zealand. His TikTok presence drives consistent discovery, with an engaged following across multiple English-speaking markets.
